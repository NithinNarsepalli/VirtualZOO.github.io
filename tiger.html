About Tiger

Tigers (Panthera tigris): Tigers are the largest of the big cats and are native to Asia. They are powerful carnivores that primarily hunt large ungulates such as deer and wild boar. Tigers are solitary hunters and are known for their distinctive orange coat with black stripes.
