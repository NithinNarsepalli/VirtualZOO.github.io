About Gorilla

Gorilla (Gorilla gorilla): Gorillas are the largest primates and are native to the forests of Central and West Africa. These herbivorous apes are primarily folivores, meaning they consume leaves, stems, and shoots, but they also eat fruit, bamboo, and occasionally insects. Gorillas live in social groups led by a dominant male called a silverback. They are known for their impressive strength and gentle nature, which has made them a beloved attraction in many zoos, where conservation efforts aim to protect these endangered animals and their habitats.
