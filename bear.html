About Bear

Bears (Various species): Bears are large omnivorous mammals found in various parts of the world. They are known for their strength and adaptability. Bears have a diverse diet that includes plant materials such as berries, nuts, and roots, as well as animal matter like fish, insects, and small mammals. They often go through periods of hyperphagia, where they eat voraciously to build up fat reserves for hibernation in the colder months.
