About Baboon

Baboon (Papio spp.): Baboons are a group of Old World monkeys found in Africa and are omnivorous. They consume a wide variety of foods, including fruits, leaves, insects, and small mammals. Baboons are known for their distinctive appearance with long dog-like snouts and a social hierarchical structure.
