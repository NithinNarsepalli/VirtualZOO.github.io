About Racoon

Raccoon (Procyon lotor): Raccoons are small omnivores native to North America. They have a highly adaptable diet, which includes fruits, vegetables, insects, small mammals, and even scavenged human food. Raccoons are known for their dexterous paws and mask-like facial markings. Their ability to thrive in urban environments makes them a familiar sight in many parts of North America
