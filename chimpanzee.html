About Chimpanzee

Chimpanzee (Pan troglodytes): Chimpanzees are highly intelligent primates native to Africa. They are omnivores with a diet that includes fruits, leaves, nuts, insects, and occasionally small vertebrates. Chimpanzees are known for their complex social structures and tool-making abilities.
