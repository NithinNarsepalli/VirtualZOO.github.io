About Zebra

Grevy's Zebra (Equus grevyi): Grevy's zebra is a distinct species of zebra native to Eastern Africa, primarily found in Kenya and Ethiopia. They are the largest and most endangered of all the zebra species. These herbivorous animals graze on a diet of grasses and occasionally browse on leaves and shrubs. Grevy's zebras are known for their unique and striking appearance, with narrow, closely spaced black stripes on a white background and large ears. They are often featured in zoos as part of conserva -tion efforts to protect this endangered species and educate the public about the importance of preserving their natural habitat.
