About Red Panda

Red Panda (Ailurus fulgens): Red pandas are small, arboreal mammals found in the Himalayan region and southwestern China. They are herbivores that primarily feed on bamboo leaves and shoots, but they also eat fruits, berries, and occasionally small rodents or insects. Red pandas are known for their striking reddish-brown fur and bushy tails. They are solitary creatures, typically active during dawn and dusk, and are classified as vulnerable due to habitat loss and poaching. Many zoos participate in breeding and conservation programs to protect these charming animals.
