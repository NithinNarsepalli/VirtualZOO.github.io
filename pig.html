About Pig

Pigs (Sus scrofa domesticus): Domestic pigs are omnivorous animals commonly found in zoos and farms around the world. They have a varied diet that can include grains, vegetables, fruits, and even small animals like insects or rodents. Pigs are highly intelligent and social animals known for their rooting behavior, which involves using their snouts to search for food.
