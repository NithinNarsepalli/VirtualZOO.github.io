About Giant Panda

Giant Panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca): Giant pandas are herbivorous bears native to China. Although they primarily eat bamboo, they are technically omnivores and may occasionally consume small animals or fish. Giant pandas are known for their unique black-and-white fur pattern and are a symbol of wildlife conservation.
