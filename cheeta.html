About Cheetah

Cheetah (Acinonyx jubatus): Cheetahs are carnivorous cats known for their incredible speed and agility. They primarily prey on small to medium-sized ungulates, such as gazelles and impalas. Cheetahs are solitary hunters and have a unique hunting style, relying on speed and stealth to catch their prey.
