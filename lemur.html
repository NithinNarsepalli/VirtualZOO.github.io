About Lemur

Ring-tailed Lemur (Lemur catta): Ring-tailed lemurs are small primates native to Madagascar. They are omnivores, with a diet that includes fruits, leaves, flowers, and occasional insects. These lemurs are recognized by their distinctive black-and-white ringed tails and are often featured in zoos due to their engaging behaviors.
