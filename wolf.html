About Wolf

Wolves (Canis lupus): Wolves are carnivorous canids that live in packs. They are highly social and cooperative hunters, primarily targeting ungulates like deer and elk.In zoos, wolves are often given diets that mimic their natural prey items to ensure their nutritional needs are met.
