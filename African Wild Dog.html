About African Wild Dog

African Wild Dog (Lycaon pictus): African wild dogs, also known as African painted dogs, are carnivorous canids found in sub-Saharan Africa. They are social pack hunters that primarily prey on antelope and other small to medium-sized ungulates. African wild dogs are known for their unique and colorful coat patterns.
