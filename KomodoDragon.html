About KomodoDragon

Komodo Dragon (Varanus komodoensis): Komodo dragons are large carnivorous reptiles native to the Indonesian islands. They are apex predators, primarily hunting deer, wild boar, and other smaller animals.Komodo dragons are known for their massive size and powerful jaws, armed with deadly bacteria-laden saliva.
