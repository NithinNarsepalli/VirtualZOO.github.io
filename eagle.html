About Eagle

Bald Eagle (Haliaeetus leucocephalus): The bald eagle is a carnivorous bird of prey native to North America. They primarily feed on fish, but they are opportunistic hunters that will also consume waterfowl and carrion. The bald eagle is a symbol of the United States and is often seen in zoos and wildlife rehabilitation centers.
