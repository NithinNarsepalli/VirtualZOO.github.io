About Lion

Lions (Panthera leo): Lions are iconic carnivorous big cats found in Africa. They are known for their hunting prowess and social structure. Lions primarily prey on large ungulates like zebras and wildebeests. In zoos,they are often fed a diet that includes meat and receive regular enrichment to mimic their natural hunting behaviors
