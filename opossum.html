About Opossum

Opossum (Didelphis virginiana): Opossums are marsupials native to North and Central America. They are opportunistic omnivores, feeding on a wide range of foods, including fruits, insects, small vertebrates, and carrion. Opossums are known for their prehensile tail and the ability to "play dead" when threatened, which is an involuntary response known as "playing possum."
